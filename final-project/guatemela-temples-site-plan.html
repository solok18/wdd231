Sacred Guatemala: Latter-day Saint Temples and Local Culture

The name brings together the spiritual beauty of the temples and the colorful traditions of Guatemalan culture.

Purpose

The goal of this site is to provide detailed information about each temple located in Guatemala. In addition to temple insights, the site will highlight local culture, traditional cuisine, and lodging options in the cities where the temples are found, helping visitors plan a meaningful and enriching stay.
