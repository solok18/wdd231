Fun Facts About Guatemala

Family and Community: Guatemalans value family and close relationships, with visiting friends and relatives being an important part of their social lives.

Traditional Clothing and textiles: Mayan textiles are a vibrant and intricate part of Guatemalan culture, with each region boasting unique patterns and techniques.

Honesty and Respect: Guatemalan society values honesty, honor, and respect for elders and authority figures.

Celebrations and Festivals: Fiestas, or celebrations, are a common form of entertainment, often featuring marimba orchestras and traditional music.

Food and Cuisine: Guatemalan cuisine is heavily influenced by Mayan traditions, with corn, chiles, and beans being staple ingredients.

Art and Music: Guatemalan art and music are influenced by both Mayan and Spanish traditions, with vibrant textiles, marimba music, and traditional dance being prominent.
